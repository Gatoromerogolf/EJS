A - Corporate Governance.


It represents the set of processes, principles, and values that govern how an organization is strategized, managed, controlled, and made transparent.

Failures in the governance system can lead to dysfunctional processes.

The corporate governance system influences what is known as the "Tone at the Top" in the Risk Appetite, and its operational-functional quality determines the management of market and process risks.

The goal is to have Effective Corporate Governance.
